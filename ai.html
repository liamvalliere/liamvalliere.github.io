Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach

Book Description:

For many, Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach is the de facto bible of artificial intelligence. It combines in-depth treatments of introductory and advanced concepts, along with historical background and accessible explanations. Including algorithms, code and pseudo-code, the book sits between master's and Ph.

The modern definition of artificial intelligence or AI is “the study and design of intelligent agents” where an intelligent agent is a system that perceives its environment and takes actions which maximise its chances of success.
