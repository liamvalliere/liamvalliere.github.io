Sans Famille

Book Description:

Sans Famille is an 1878 French novel by Hector Malot. The most recent English translation is Alone in the World by Adrian de Bruyn, 2007. The novel was reportedly inspired by the Italian street musicians of the 19th century, in particular the harpists from Viggiano, Basilicata.

The story has a happy ending: Rémi finds his biological family, and discovers he is the heir to a fortune, enabling him to help those who previously helped him.
