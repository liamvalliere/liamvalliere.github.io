Where The WILD THINGS ARE

Book Description:

The book tells the story of Max, a rambunctious and sensitive boy who feels misunderstood at home and escapes to where the Wild Things are. Max lands on an island where he meets mysterious and strange creatures whose emotions are as wild and unpredictable as their actions.

The book tells the story of a child who is punished, who creates his own world in which he feels loved as he would like, only to discover that he wants to return to the family context, being welcomed again warmly.
